About Choice Blend

Choice Blend Studio has been in business since 2007 in the West Kendall area of Miami, Florida. Known as a popular rehearsal spot for many artists and bands in the Miami-area, we also provide top-notch recording and mixing services. Choice Blend provides a comfortable, professional studio environment, whether you're looking for a place to record your next project or a to rehearse for a couple of hours.
